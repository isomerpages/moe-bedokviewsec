<h2>School Crest</h2>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:50%" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/BVSS%20logo.png">
</div>
<p>The Lighthouse is our school symbol and is depicted on our school crest.
It represents the fire of passion and guiding light to direct our thoughts
and actions.</p>
<p>A strong and steadfast monument resting on a solid foundation, it is able
to withstand the waves of change and challenges. It also symbolises our
aspirations to achieve high standards of integrity, strong character, and
resilience.</p>
<h2>School Colours</h2>
<p>Blue: Symbolises an endless quest for knowledge and character development
<br>Beige: Represents determination and joy in achieving our dreams</p>
