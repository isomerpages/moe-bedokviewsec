<h4><strong><u>Published DSA categories and criteria</u></strong></h4>
<h4><strong>Sports Talent Areas</strong></h4>
<p><strong>Offered Sports</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Badminton (Boys)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Basketball (Boys &amp; Girls)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Football (Boys)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Netball (Girls)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Track &amp; Field (Boys &amp; Girls)</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Selection Criteria</strong>
<br>Applicants should demonstrate the following:
<br>i. <strong>Strong passion and aptitude</strong> in a specific sport. Students
must demonstrate a relatively high level of skill and interest in the sport
they are applying for.
<br>ii. <strong>CCA participation and achievements</strong> — preferably represented
their school at the <strong>National School Games (NSG)</strong> level or
equivalent platforms.
<br>iii. <strong>Academic performance</strong> of at least <strong>50% overall</strong> for
Primary 5 and Primary 6 results.
<br>iv. <strong>Conduct Grade</strong> of at least <em>Very Good</em>.
<br>v. <strong>Relevant sporting experience and achievements</strong> will be
considered, where available.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Selection Process</strong>
<br>Shortlisted applicants will be required to attend an <strong>interview and selection trials</strong>.
<br>
</p>

<h4><strong>Key Dates &amp; Information</strong></h4>
<p><strong>🗓️ Application Period</strong>
<br><strong>6 May 2026 (11:00am) – 2 June 2026 (4:30pm)</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Applications must be submitted via the online DSA-Sec Portal.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Application is <strong>free of charge</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>For more details, please visit the MOE website: <a href="http://www.moe.gov.sg/dsa-sec" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_new">www.moe.gov.sg/dsa-sec</a>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<hr>
<p><strong>📩 Shortlisting Notification</strong>
<br><strong>By 26 June 2026</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Parents will be notified if their child has been shortlisted for DSA selection.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<hr>
<p><strong>🎯 Trials, Auditions and/or Interviews</strong>
<br><strong>29 June – 28 August 2026</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Shortlisted applicants will attend trials, auditions and/or interviews
at <strong>Bedok View Secondary School</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<hr>
<p><strong>📢 Application Outcomes</strong>
<br><strong>Between 17 August and 28 August 2026</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>All applicants will receive the outcome of their DSA applications.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<hr>
<p><strong>📝 School Preference Submission</strong>
<br><strong>19 October 2026 (9:00am) – 23 October 2026 (4:30pm)</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Students with at least one DSA offer (including from SOTA) will receive
an SMS and email via Postman.gov.sg</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Only <strong>one parent</strong> is required to log in to the DSA-Sec Portal
to submit school preferences.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Parents who are unable to submit online may approach their child’s primary
school for assistance.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<hr>
<p><strong>🎓 Final Results Release</strong>
<br><strong>By End November 2026</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Students in MOE mainstream primary schools will receive their DSA-Sec
results together with their PSLE results.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<hr>
<p><strong>Important Notes</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Parents and students are encouraged to choose schools wisely based on
the students’ aptitudes and strengths, bearing in mind the schools’ academic
and non-academic requirements, and the programmes available to develop
the area of talent.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Applicants who do not have prior experience may also apply. The school
will assess applicants based on the selection criteria, which may include
assessing the potential of the applicants.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Meeting all the criteria does not guarantee the student will be shortlisted/given
an offer.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students who are successfully admitted to the school via DSA are required
to honour their commitment to the school and participate in the activities
related to the talent they are selected for from Sec 1 to Sec 4.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
